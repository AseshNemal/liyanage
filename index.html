    Orange

    Orange Electric is Orel Corporation’s flagship brand and a market leading brand in Sri Lanka. The brand is synonymous with quality and trust whilst being respected throughout Sri Lankan households and largescale projects alike.

    Kalani

    Kelani Cables Ltd. was founded in 1969, as an import-substitute manufacturer during the controlled-economy era. Initially, components such as drawn wire were imported, but over time, the company commenced the manufacture of this commodity at its factory. Hence, Kelani is the pioneer in Sri Lanka’s wire drawing industry.

    Nippon Paint

    Nippon Paint “The Paint Expert” which is better known worldwide for its superior Japanese technology on decorative, automotive and industrial coatings solutions are now in Sri Lanka to revolutionized the paints and coatings industry by providing sustainable and environmental friendly coatings solutions.

    Kevilton

    Kevilton provides comprehensive electrical product solutions for your residential, office or industrial spaces, while taking every effort to make electrical energy usage safe and efficient, to deliver on our brand promise of being “Your 1st Choice”.

    S_LON

    ACL

    Commencing its operation in 2014 ACL Electric was formed in manufacturing & marketing high quality electrical switches, sockets, breakers and accessories. This complied with the company objective to move ACL Cables PLC in a bid to strengthen its value chain and to become a comprehensive one-stop shop for electrical requirements.
