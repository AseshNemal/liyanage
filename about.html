Our Story

A family-run hardware shop serving Minuwangoda for over 30 years

Liyanage Trade Center started as a small neighbourhood hardware store and has grown into a trusted local supplier of paints, electricals and plumbing materials. We focus on practical advice, fast service and stocking trusted brands so your project keeps moving.

Visit our shop at 152 Colombo Road for colour matching, immediate pickups, and bulk order support. For quick estimates use our Calculator page or contact us directly from the Contact page.
