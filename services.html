Orange

Orange Electric is Orel Corporation’s flagship brand and a market-leading brand in Sri Lanka. The brand is synonymous with quality and trust, respected throughout Sri Lankan households and large-scale projects alike.

Kalani

Kelani Cables Ltd. was founded in 1969 as an import-substitute manufacturer during the controlled-economy era. Initially, components such as drawn wire were imported, but over time, the company commenced manufacturing this commodity at its factory. Hence, Kelani is the pioneer in Sri Lanka’s wire drawing industry.

Nippon Paint

Nippon Paint, “The Paint Expert,” known worldwide for its superior Japanese technology in decorative, automotive, and industrial coatings solutions, is now in Sri Lanka to revolutionize the paints and coatings industry by providing sustainable and environmentally friendly coatings solutions.

Kevilton

Kevilton provides comprehensive electrical product solutions for your residential, office, or industrial spaces, while taking every effort to make electrical energy usage safe and efficient, delivering on our brand promise of being “Your 1st Choice.”

S_LON

ACL

Commencing its operation in 2014, ACL Electric was formed to manufacture and market high-quality electrical switches, sockets, breakers, and accessories. This complied with the company objective to move ACL Cables PLC in a bid to strengthen its value chain and become a comprehensive one-stop shop for electrical requirements.
